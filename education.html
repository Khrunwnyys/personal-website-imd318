My Education Journey


My education began at Tadika Kemas, where I started preschool at the age of 4. I continued with primary school,
where I had a strong start to my schooling journey. In primary school, I developed a passion for learning activities
that allowed me to use both my hands and logic.

In high school, my interest in art and design began to surface. I chose art and design subjects, and in Form 5, I
was required to complete coursework in this area. Although it seemed challenging at first, I unexpectedly
enjoyed the process and was very satisfied with the outcome of my work.

After completing high school, I pursued further studies and enrolled in a Diploma in Information Management at
UiTM Machang Campus, Kelantan Branch. During this time, through various courses, internships, and extracurricular
activities, I was able to acquire a range of valuable skills that have shaped my learning journey and career path.
